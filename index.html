OBC 2025 Six Nations total points scored competition

Introduction

Welcome to the Old Barn Club (OBC) 2025 Six Nations total points competition!

This is your chance to test your knowledge and predict the total number of points scored by all the teams over 15 games. There are 4 prizes for cloese entries.

So what are you waiting for? Make your prediction.

France and Wales start the 2025 Six Nations when they meet in Paris at the Stade de France on Friday night the 31st of January.

Rules

1, Nearest the total points scored wins!

2, There should be 4 prizes for the 62 entries and the prize money has been broken down as follows. : £165 for 1st prize, £85 for 2nd prize, £40 for 3rd prize and £20 for the 4th prize.

3, If two entries are closes to a prize then the next prize is included. i.e. if 2nd place is equal between 2 entries then (£85+£40) £125 will be split between the two entries. If the 4th prize is shared then the 2 entries will split the £20.

To verify the results in the spreadsheet the totals will be compared to the Wiki 2025 Six nations Website. The link below takes you to the Wiki RWC2025 website.Sports_Rugby

About

This website is under development and will hopefully support the OBC 2026 Six Nations total points competition. The Web app has been developed so that some parts can be reused for the annual spring 6 Nations total points scored competition.

The website's spreadsheet should be updated after the day's game(s) have been played. The daily update of data will allow the contestants to view the results more frequently instead of waiting until after the weekend to view the results and statistics on the OBC Sports bar notice board.

The website originally included much more functionality but has now been simplified to just support the results/statistics and information links. The 6 Nations version will allow the administrator to update the required data after the matches instead of after end of the day.

If you have any new requirements for the website please speak to Aubrey. Depending on their value and time they maybe included.Sports_Rugby
